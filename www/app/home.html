Understand Yourself Through YouTube. Dive Deeper with @Social AI Profile.

Intrigued by the videos you watch but unsure what they reveal about you? Telegram Bot @Social AI Profile takes you on a journey of self-discovery through the lens of your YouTube habits. This secure Telegram bot crafts a personalized description based on your recent viewing preferences, offering a glimpse into your interests and personality.

Here's how it works:

  • Connect your YouTube account: We use secure YouTube OAuth to access your liked and disliked videos (only the 5 most recent). We never store any personal information like username, email, or any data from YouTube.
  • Let Gemini describe you: We send this anonymized data to the powerful Gemini AI provided by Google, which crafts your Social Profile based on your preferences.
  • Discover your hidden side: Get a glimpse into your interests and personality based on your YouTube choices.

What's important to know:

  • This bot is completely anonymous. We don't store any user data, neither your personal data nor your YouTube preferences.
  • This bot is all about fun and self-discovery.
